Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in the northern part of India. It is situated in the state of Himachal Pradesh and is known for its stunning natural beauty, snow-capped mountains, and adventure activities.


Things to do in Manali

  1. Hiking in the mountains
    • Explore the famous Rohtang Pass
    • Take a trek to Hampta Pass
  2. Visit the hot springs
    • Take a dip in Vashisht Kund
    • Relax in the Manikaran hot springs
  3. Enjoy adventure sports
    • Try paragliding in Solang Valley
    • Go river rafting in the Beas River
  4. Explore local markets
    • Visit the Mall Road for shopping and food
    • Check out the Old Manali Market for handicrafts and souvenirs
  5. Visit nearby attractions
    • Explore the historic town of Naggar
    • Take a day trip to the scenic town of Kasol
